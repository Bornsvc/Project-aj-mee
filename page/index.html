International Conference on Natural Science and Engineering (ICNSE 2024)

Date: November 4–6, 2024

Location: Faculty of Engineering, National University of Laos, Vientiane Capital, Lao PDR

Theme: Fostering Science, Technology and Innovation for Sustainable Development in Lao PDR and Beyond

Objectives

  • Strengthen collaboration between academics, researchers, and industries.
  • Provide a forum for exchanging ideas and research.
  • Promote science and technology development in Laos and the region.
  • Highlight innovations for sustainable development.

Topics of Interest

  1. Advanced Materials and Manufacturing
  2. Environmental Engineering and Climate Change
  3. Civil and Infrastructure Engineering
  4. Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency
  5. Electrical and Electronic Systems
  6. Information and Communication Technology
  7. Mechanical and Mechatronics Engineering
  8. STEM Education and Engineering Education
  9. Water Resource Management
  10. Engineering Policy and Innovation Management
